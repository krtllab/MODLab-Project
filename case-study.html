THE BRIEF.

Let’s bring your darkest visions to life. I specialize in highly detailed, meticulously crafted digital illustrations tailored for the heavy music scene, streetwear brands, and dark fantasy publications. Every piece is 100% original, human-crafted, and designed to leave a lasting impact.

My mandate was simple: fix the first-hour experience without rebuilding the product from scratch. The budget was fixed and the engineering team had six weeks to ship.

"We're losing users in the first 20 minutes. They sign up, see the dashboard, and just bounce. The product does exactly what they need — they just can't find it."

Discovery & research

User interviews

I ran 9 user interviews with a mix of current paying customers and recently churned trial users. The sessions were 45-minute moderated usability tests combined with post-task interviews. Key findings:

  • 7 of 9 users couldn't locate the "Workflows" section without help during their first session
  • The notification log and the analytics panel were confused for one another by 5 users
  • The sidebar had 14 top-level navigation items — users reported feeling "unsure where to start"
  • Trial users specifically wanted a "get your first notification live" path, not a feature overview

Heuristic audit

I ran a full Nielsen heuristic evaluation of the existing interface and identified 23 violations across 6 categories. The most critical were around visibility of system status, recognition over recall, and error prevention in the workflow builder.

MODLab Project — illustration & UI design for brands that refuse to blend in.

Progressive disclosure

Show only what's needed for the current task. Advanced features revealed on demand, not upfront.

Opinionated navigation

Reduce the sidebar to 6 core items. Group secondary actions in context panels rather than global nav.

Goal-first onboarding

Replace the feature tour with a 3-step "send your first notification" flow that delivers an aha-moment in under 5 minutes.

The Work Flow (how we work)

to ensure the best result and a smooth collaboration, I follow a strict, structured process:

Briefing & Consultation

We start by discussing your vision. You provide the project details, core concepts, and any visual references. Once we agree on the scope and timeline, a 50% upfront deposit is required to secure your slot.

Rough Sketch & Composition

I will create a rough sketch focusing on the layout, anatomy, and overall composition. This is the stage where we make sure the foundation is solid before I commit to the heavy detailing.

Inking & High-Detailing.

Once the sketch is approved, I move on to the intricate linework and shading. Because my style relies heavily on meticulous details, major compositional changes cannot be made during this stage.

Coloring & Final polishing

If your project includes color, I will apply the atmospheric tones, highlights, and textures to bring the artwork to its final, brutal form.

Final Delivery

Upon your final approval and the clearance of the remaining 50% payment, I will send you the high-resolution source files (PSD/TIFF/PNG/JPG) formatted and ready for print or digital use.
